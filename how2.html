How to Use Deep Edge

Step-by-step guides for measuring lawns, generating quotes, managing jobs, and more. Designed for NZ landscapers and lawn mowers.

How to Quote with the AI Tool

Deep Edge's AI measures lawns, roofs, and gutters instantly from an address or photo, helping you quote faster.

  1. Log in and click New Quote from the dashboard.
  2. Enter the customer's address (e.g., "67 Williamson Ave, Grey Lynn, Auckland").
  3. Click AI Measure — the tool pulls satellite data for lawn area (m²), roof size, and gutter length.
  4. Adjust if needed (e.g., exclude driveways) and add notes.
  5. Review the auto-calculated quote based on your pricing settings.
Tip: Upload a phone photo for even more accuracy on irregular lawns.

How to Generate a Quote

Create professional quotes in under a minute, complete with measurements and pricing.

  1. After measuring, click Generate Quote.
  2. Select services (e.g., mowing, edging, gutter clean) — prices apply automatically from your settings.
  3. Add extras like travel fees, GST, or discounts.
  4. Customize the template with your logo, terms, and payment options.
  5. Preview and save — the quote is ready to send!
Tip: Use templates for repeat jobs to save even more time.

How to Manage Your Site

Customize Deep Edge to fit your business workflow, including pricing and branding.

  1. Go to Settings > Site Management.
  2. Upload your logo and update business details (name, address, phone).
  3. Set default pricing (e.g., $5/m² for mowing, $15/m for gutters).
  4. Customize quote/invoice templates with colors, fonts, and terms.
  5. Enable integrations (e.g., Xero for accounting or Stripe for payments).
Tip: Set up notifications for new quotes or job updates.

How to Change Job Status

Track jobs from quote to complete for better organization.

  1. Go to Jobs Dashboard and select a job.
  2. Click the status dropdown (e.g., "Quoted" → "Accepted").
  3. Update with notes, attachments, or assignee (if multi-user).
  4. Click Save — status changes trigger notifications if set up.
  5. Once "Completed", convert to invoice automatically.
Tip: Use colors for quick visual tracking (e.g., green for paid).

How to Use the Route Planner

Optimize your day with smart routing for multiple jobs.

  1. Go to Route Planner from the dashboard.
  2. Add jobs from your list or enter addresses manually.
  3. Click Optimize Route — AI suggests the fastest order based on traffic and distance.
  4. Adjust stops or add breaks, then export to Google Maps or print.
  5. Save routes for recurring clients (e.g., weekly mows).
Tip: Integrate with your calendar for automatic scheduling.

How to Send a Quote

Share quotes instantly via email, SMS, or link.

  1. From the quote preview, click Send Quote.
  2. Enter customer's email/phone or shareable link.
  3. Add a personal message (e.g., "Quote for your Grey Lynn lawn — any questions?").
  4. Click Send — track opens/accepts in your dashboard.
  5. If accepted, it auto-updates job status.
Tip: Quotes expire after 7 days by default — customize in settings.

How to Check Your Plan Info

View usage, billing, and upgrade options anytime.

  1. Go to Settings > Billing & Plan.
  2. See your current plan (e.g., Launch: 20 quotes/month), quotes used, and renewal date.
  3. Compare plans side-by-side to upgrade (e.g., to Performance for more quotes).
  4. Update payment details or cancel if needed.
  5. Contact support if you need a custom plan.
Tip: Get alerts when you're nearing quote limits.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the AI accurate for NZ conditions?

Yes, it's tuned for Kiwi lawns with high-res NZ data. Handles slopes, wet weather, and urban sections — 95–98% accuracy reported.

Can I integrate with Xero or Stripe?

Absolutely! Connect in Settings > Integrations for seamless invoicing and payments.

What if I exceed my quote limit?

You'll get a warning — upgrade anytime, or overage quotes are $2 each on Launch/Performance plans.

Yes, fully responsive on iOS/Android — or use our PWA for offline access.
